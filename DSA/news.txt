GROUND SOFTWARE ENGINEER

As a Ground Software Engineer, you will be working with engineers around the company to develop software solutions for complex engineering problems. Successful candidates will be able to design software systems quickly, work alone or with other experienced developers, produce high quality code, constructively review code written by other team members and be comfortable knowing their code is a major part of our success.
